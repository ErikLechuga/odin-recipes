Enfrijoladas

Enfrijoladas are made by frying corn tortillas in oil, and then letting them soak in beans.

Ingredients
  • Tortillas
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Favorite Kind of beans
  • Cheese
Steps
  1. First, heat up your beans and oil.
  2. Next, fry your tortilla on each side for around 30 seconds.
  3. Then, dip your tortilla into your beans. Beans should be slightly watery.
  4. Finally, place the soaked tortilla on a plate, and roll it up. You can roll chicken into the tortilla, and place cheese on top.
